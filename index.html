About East Anglian Air Ambulance

For the past 18 years, East Anglian Air Ambulance has attended over 22,000 missions, saving lives and giving people a second chance.

A single mission costs ~£3,500 and without your help, they couldn’t keep flying.

How you can help

£10

Buys a 12-pack of Transpore tape, a hypo-allergenic surgical tape.

£30

Buys Celox gauze, used to protect wounds and stop bleeding from life threatening injuries.

£100

Buys a 10-pack of Disposable pressure infusors, used to drain fluid out quickly and allows for rapid IV infusion.

Did you know? £200 buys enough fuel to fly one of their helicopters for 1 hour.
